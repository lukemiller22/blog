I'm Luke - Christian, husband, father of three, and co-founder at Buildonline. I care about words, quiet disciplines, and the hard work of building things that last.

This site is my small attempt to tend the digital commons through writing - essays, book reviews, and other experiments in structure and form. I also wanted a place to track books I've read and link to personal projects I'm working on. There is a glossary of concepts I return to often and a collection of writing selections that inspire me to read, write, and live.

There is more about me if you're curious. Plus photos from life on the road and at home.
