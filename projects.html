Token & Thread

Hyperlink notebook of meaning in motion. It explores how small units of thought (e.g. claims, metaphors, questions, stories) combine to form larger patterns of understanding. At its core, there is a custom framework of 118 elements (recurring tokens of discourse) and the threads that link them across texts, disciplines, and experiences. This is not a finished theory but a living record of attention. It's my attempt to notice what holds weight, what connects, and what matters.
